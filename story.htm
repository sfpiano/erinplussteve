The story of Erin & Steve

1980s

All great stories have to start somewhere and this one begins in the 80s, a decade of wild colors, parachute pants, Top Gun and New Kids on the Block.

2003

Erin (band) and Steve (choir) perform at the Philadelphia Thanksgiving parade unbeknownst to each other.

2004

Time for college! Erin heads west to IUP while Steve heads north to study at RIT.

2005

Steve realizes he made a mistake and transfers to UMD.

2009

After graduating Erin enters the working world for a brief time to save up for graduate school.

Meanwhile Steve moves to Virginia to begin his career at General Dynamics.

2010

Erin enrolls in the graduate program at UNCG for flute performance.

May 2013

Erin moves to DC for an internship.

June 2013

After a series of brief interactions online Steve and Erin have their first date at La Tasca in DC! Steve arrives two hours early and paces around the block repeatedly.

August 2013

Erin and Steve become an official couple.

December 2015

Steve proposes at the tidal basin, Erin says yes, the engagement begins!

October 15, 2016

Erin and Steve get hitched!
