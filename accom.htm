Heritage Hills Golf Resort

The Heritage Hills Golf Resort is a 10 minute drive from the church. Check in time is 3pm, check out time is 11am.

To make a reservation please call 717-755-0123 ext. 1580 or email the reservations department at bookings@hhgr.com.

A discounted rate is available starting at $160 per night by booking under the Moscony/Fiorelli wedding. If booking two or more nights there is an additional discount of $139+tax for a standard room.

Please make sure to reserve your room by August 30th to ensure your spot in our room block. After this point the rooms currently set aside for our event will be made available to the general public.

Directions

From Points North (Harrisburg): Take I-83 south to Exit 18, Mount Rose Avenue. At the exit, turn left onto Mount Rose Avenue (Route 124 East). Proceed for 1 mile to the resort located on the right.

From Points South (Baltimore): Take I-83 north to Exit 18, Mount Rose Avenue. Off the exit bear to the right and proceed for 1 mile to the resort located on the right.

From Points West (Gettysburg): Take Route 30 East to I-83 South. Follow I-83 South to Exit 18, Mount Rose Avenue. At the exit traffic light, turn left onto Mount Rose Avenue (Route 124 East). Proceed for 1 mile to the resort located on the right.
Or
Take Route 30 East and continue to the Memory Lane exit. Off the exit bear right and continue through 5 traffic lights to Prospect Road (Route 124 East). Turn left and proceed east for 1 mile to the resort the right.
